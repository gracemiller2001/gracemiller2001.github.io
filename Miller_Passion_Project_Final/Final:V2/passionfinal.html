Beginning Spanish Language Learning;

Driving conjugation, content, and culture into the everyday.

Compiled by Grace Miller

Beginning Spanish Language Learning is an online resource page to connect and learn the Spanish language and culture from anywhere. Founder, Grace Miller, is a Visual Communication and Spanish student at Ohio University. Constantly inspired to improve her langauge skills, she has compiled a list of resources/lesson plans to learn about these aspects of the language and culture yourself with a compilation of multimedia-based resources. Please note that the website's content, in addition to much of the external content, is produced in English, as the program lessons are directed towards beginners. However, much of external content, such as the television and/or podcast series can be changed manually to either language (Spanish or English).
